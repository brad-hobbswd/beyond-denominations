About the Author

Bradley Hobbs is a Christian author, educator, and lifelong student of Scripture who is passionate about helping readers return to the simplicity and beauty of the apostolic witness. His writing combines thoughtful reflection with careful study, inviting ordinary people to explore the Bible for themselves and discover the enduring message revealed through Jesus Christ.

Rather than approaching Scripture through the lens of tradition or denominational history, Bradley encourages readers to begin where the New Testament begins, allowing the Book of Acts and the apostles to establish the pattern for faith, discipleship, and the life of the church.

His books are written with the conviction that truth should be accessible, beautifully presented, and firmly rooted in the Word of God. Each project is designed to encourage careful reading, personal study, and a renewed appreciation for the gospel proclaimed by the first believers.

Beyond Denominations reflects that same desire, extending an invitation to rediscover the church through the apostolic doctrine, the revelation of Jesus Christ, and the transforming work of the Holy Ghost.

"Search the scriptures; for in them ye think ye have eternal life: and they are they which testify of me."
